We build from the heart

At PMA Renovations, we believe comfort, safety, and beauty are the pillars of a happy and healthy home.

Walking into your space and feeling comfortable with the design you love, safe with a job well done, and captivated by the results is priceless. We strive to give you that feeling.

  • We are general contractors, but we are humans and fellow homeowners first. We understand what you’re going through, and we support you every step of the way.
  • With decades of experience, the team at PMA Renovations is trained to build stunning homes–and will go above and beyond to make you happy.
  • You want to be heard, so we always pay attention. If you have a special request, we adjust. Our business thrives because our clients know we don’t just make promises–we honor them.
  • By putting your trust in PMA Renovations, you’re welcoming a team of individuals who care about your home as much as you do.

Meet Ofir

I first started working in construction as a kid at my father’s electrical company, while always dreaming one day to own my own multiple trade construction company.

Working with my father allowed me to absorb the knowledge and work ethic at such a young age.

  • I realized I had the heart to change people’s perceptions of general contractors as “uncaring” and “inefficient.” The kind of heart that wants to give back to others.
  • The kind of heart that will answer your call at 10 p.m. so you can brainstorm an idea with me. So, that’s how PMA Renovations was born.

I built this company from the ground up, and we’ve grown because every PMA team member shares my love for this industry and the people we serve.

I'm always learning and constantly evolving. If you decide to take this journey with my company and me, I will do everything in my power to bring your vision to life–on time and on budget.
